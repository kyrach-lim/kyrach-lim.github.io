BIO

Kyra Chhiu-Lim is a Digital Media Art student at San Jose State University exploring various mediums of digital media including video, web design, mixed media, and installation. To create her art, she utilizes tools such as adobe premiere, photoshop, atom, and illustrator. Kyra addresses subjects relating to femininity, surveillance, and the 21st century's fast growing technology.

In her project "Femininity: A How-to Video", Kyra pokes at steoreotypes womxn face and how society expects them to adhere to their idealistic views. In other projects, Kyra addresses the issues that technology brings in our advanced society, commenting on how surveillance is becoming more widespread and invading our privacy. On the topic of technology, Kyra has also made a project about robots and how the combination of their high intelligence and uncanny resemeblance to humans can create fear for those living among them.
